The problem

Where hospitality finance breaks down

Fragmented systems

PMS, POS, and back-office systems each hold a piece of the truth — and none of them talk to each other. Finance reconciles by hand what should already be in the GL.

Month-end close overhead

Accruals, intercompany postings, and reconciliations still depend on manual steps. A 10-day close is not a resourcing problem — it's an automation gap.

Delayed financial insights

When reporting runs weeks behind operations, owners decide from the past. In a market that moves on RevPAR and ADR daily, that delay costs real money.

Slow adoption to new tech

Every new property or country means rebuilding from scratch. Rigid architectures turn growth into IT projects — right when the business should accelerate.

Hotel 360° is a certified Oracle NetSuite application purpose-built for hospitality groups. It automates the full financial cycle — from PMS to POS transactions and into the back office GL reporting layer — supporting multi-property, multi-country portfolios. It ships with pre-built integrations, a USALI v12 reporting framework, hospitality-specific workflows, and a business consolidation logic that supports unique industry requirements.

A generic ERP deployment is a blank canvas. Hotel 360° ships with pre-built integrations, USALI v12 reporting, hospitality workflows, and multi-property consolidation already in place — so you go live with a system designed for your industry, not one you have to build from scratch. Typical implementation ranges from 3–6 months versus 9 to 12 months for custom build models.

Hotel 360° automates transaction processing and reconciliation so that data from front-of-house systems lands in the general ledger automatically, every day.
